Privacy Policy — Measure AR

Last updated: January 2025

Measure AR is developed by Lakvanta Technologies. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains how Measure AR handles information.

1. Overview

Measure AR is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, sell, or share personal data.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Measure AR does not collect:

  • Personal information such as name, email, or phone number
  • Camera photos or video recordings
  • Measurement data or AR scans
  • Location data
  • Usage data linked to your identity

3. Camera Usage

Measure AR requires camera access only to detect real-world surfaces and calculate measurements using Apple ARKit.

Camera input is processed in real time on your device. No images or video frames are saved, uploaded, or transmitted.

4. Measurement Data

All measurements (length, area, shapes) are calculated locally. If you choose to save measurements, they remain stored only on your device.

Lakvanta Technologies has no access to your measurement data.

5. Analytics & Diagnostics

Measure AR may use Apple-provided system diagnostics such as crash reports to improve stability and performance.

These diagnostics are anonymous and do not include personal data, camera content, or measurement information.

6. Third-Party Services

Measure AR does not use advertising networks, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking services.

The app relies only on Apple system frameworks such as ARKit.

7. Children’s Privacy

Measure AR does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of child data misuse.

8. Data Security

Because Measure AR does not transmit or store data on external servers, your information remains under your control at all times.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will be published on this page.
