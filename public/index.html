Privacy Policy — elitePDF

A quick overview of how we handle your data. For full details, see the standalone Privacy page.

Summary

elitePDF is built with a privacy-first mindset. Our core principles:

  • Your PDFs and documents stay on your device or your own cloud accounts.
  • We don’t sell your data, and we don’t inspect the content of your files.
  • Analytics, where used, are focused on crashes and performance — not your content.

Files & Documents

Files are processed on-device whenever possible. If you connect platforms like Google Drive, Dropbox, OneDrive or iCloud, your documents move between your device and those providers under their own privacy policies.
