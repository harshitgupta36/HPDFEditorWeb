Privacy Policy

App: TinyGenius Hub · Last updated: May 19, 2026 · Contact: tinygeniushubapp@gmail.com

TinyGenius Hub ("we," "us," or "our") provides a learning app for children ages 1–9 with parent-controlled accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the TinyGenius Hub iOS app.

1. Who this policy applies to

  • Parents and guardians create and manage the only sign-in accounts in the app.
  • Children do not create their own accounts. Learner profiles are created and managed by the parent.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Parent account information

When you sign up or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email and password, or Apple ID data when you use Sign in with Apple
  • Parent display name (optional)
  • Parent lock PIN, stored as a one-way hash

2.2 Learner profile information

  • Name or nickname, age (1–9), optional gender, avatar, and learning progress

2.3 Support messages

If you use Contact TinyGenius, we store your name, email, message, and account id to respond.

2.4 Technical data

  • Diagnostic app events (not used for third-party advertising)
  • On-device preferences and optional "Remember me" (Keychain on iPhone)

3. How we use information

  • Provide and sync profiles and progress
  • Authenticate parents and protect learner switching
  • Operate activities, rewards, and age-appropriate content
  • Respond to support requests and improve security

We do not sell personal information or use learner data for third-party ads.

4. Where data is stored

  • Cloud: Supabase (PostgreSQL) when you sign in
  • On device: Local cache and Keychain for convenience

5. Sharing with third parties

ProviderPurpose
SupabaseAuth, database, storage
AppleSign in with Apple, App Store

6. Children's privacy (COPPA)

We collect child profile data only from parents. Children cannot register on their own. Parents may delete profiles or the entire account in the app.

7. Data retention and deletion

  • Data remains while your account is active
  • Delete account in Family settings removes cloud data when deletion succeeds

8. Security

We use HTTPS/TLS, database row-level security, and hashed PINs. No storage method is 100% secure.

9. Your rights

Contact tinygeniushubapp@gmail.com or use in-app account deletion.

10. International users

Data may be processed where our servers are located.

11. Changes

We may update this policy; continued use means acceptance.
