Privacy Policy

App: TinyGenius

Last updated: May 19, 2026

Contact: tinygeniushubapp@gmail.com

TinyGenius ("we," "us," or "our") provides a learning app for children ages 1-9 with parent-controlled accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the TinyGenius iOS app.

1. Who this policy applies to

  • Parents and guardians create and manage the only sign-in accounts in the app.
  • Children do not create their own accounts. Learner profiles (name, age, avatar, progress) are created and managed by the parent under the parent account.

If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

2. Information we collect

2.1 Parent account information

When you sign up or sign in, we may collect:

  • Email address and password (email sign-in), or Apple ID token data when you use Sign in with Apple (Apple may share your name or email only on first sign-in, per Apple's rules)
  • Parent display name (optional)
  • Parent lock PIN, stored as a one-way hash (we do not store your PIN in plain text)

2.2 Learner profile information

For each child profile you add, we may store:

  • First name or nickname
  • Age (1-9) and derived learning level
  • Gender selection (optional)
  • Avatar icon and color preference
  • Learning progress (stars, activities, assignments, and related stats)

2.3 Support messages

If you use Contact TinyGenius, we store your name, email, message, and parent account identifier so we can respond.

2.4 Technical and usage data

  • App events (e.g. sign-in, sync errors) may be logged for troubleshooting. In production builds, these are primarily used for diagnostics, not advertising.
  • Device-stored preferences (e.g. last selected learner, "Remember me") on your iPhone

2.5 "Remember me"

If you enable Remember me, your email is stored locally and your password is stored in the iOS Keychain on your device only, so you can sign in faster. You can turn this off by disabling Remember me or signing out.

3. How we use information

We use information to:

  • Provide and sync learner profiles and progress across your devices
  • Authenticate parents and protect learner switching with a parent PIN
  • Operate learning activities, rewards, and age-appropriate content
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve reliability and security of the app

We do not sell personal information. We do not use learner data for third-party advertising.

4. Where data is stored

  • Cloud: When you sign in, data is stored in our Supabase (PostgreSQL) project, hosted in the region you chose when creating the project. Supabase acts as our data processor.
  • On device: Some data is cached locally (UserDefaults and Keychain) for offline use and faster launch.

5. Sharing with third parties

We share data only as needed to run the app:

ProviderPurpose SupabaseAuthentication, database, and file storage for profiles and progress AppleSign in with Apple (if you choose it) and App Store distribution

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security.

6. Children's privacy (COPPA)

TinyGenius is designed for use by children under parent supervision. We collect child profile information only from the parent who controls the account. We do not knowingly allow children to register directly or provide contact information without parental involvement.

Parents can review, update, or delete learner profiles in the app and delete the entire parent account (which removes associated cloud data when deletion succeeds).

7. Data retention and deletion

  • Data remains while your account is active.
  • Delete account in Family settings removes your parent account and associated learners from the cloud when deletion completes successfully.
  • Support messages may be retained for a reasonable period to handle inquiries.

8. Security

We use industry-standard practices including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), row-level security on the database so each parent only accesses their own data, and hashed parent PINs. No method of storage is 100% secure; please use a strong password and keep your device PIN/biometrics enabled.

9. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal information. To exercise these rights, contact support@tinygeniusworld.com or use in-app account deletion.

10. International users

If you use the app outside your home country, information may be processed in the country where our servers are located.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will post the new date at the top and, where appropriate, notify you in the app. Continued use after changes means you accept the updated policy.
