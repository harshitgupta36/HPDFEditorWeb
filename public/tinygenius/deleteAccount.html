Delete Your Account & Data

TinyGenius — published by TinyGenius World

This page explains how parents can request deletion of their TinyGenius account and all associated data. TinyGenius is a children's learning app; accounts are created and managed by a parent or guardian.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (instant)

  1. Open TinyGenius and go to the Parent area (enter your parent PIN if asked).
  2. Open Settings → Account.
  3. Tap Delete Account and confirm.
  4. Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted right away.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can’t access the app, email us from the address used to create your account:

  • Email: support@tinygeniusworld.com
  • Subject: “Delete my account”
  • We verify ownership and delete your account within 30 days.

What data is deleted

DataActionRetention
Parent account (email / sign-in)DeletedRemoved immediately

Assignment / activity recordsDeletedRemoved immediately
What we keep: We do not retain personal data after deletion. Anonymized, non-identifying records may be kept only where required by law, and any backups containing your data are purged within 90 days.

If you have questions, contact support@tinygeniusworld.com.
