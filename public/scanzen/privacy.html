Privacy Policy — Scanzen

Last updated: June 16, 2026

Scanzen is developed by Lakvanta Technologies. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your data. This Privacy Policy explains how Scanzen handles information.

1. Overview

Scanzen is a document scanner designed to work directly on your device. You do not need an account to use it. We do not collect, store, sell, or share your personal data or the documents you scan.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

Scanzen does not collect:

  • Personal information such as name, email, or phone number
  • The documents, photos, or pages you scan or import
  • Recognized (OCR) text or the files you export
  • Location data
  • Usage data linked to your identity

3. Camera Usage

Scanzen requests camera access only so you can scan documents, ID cards, books, and QR codes. Camera input is processed in real time on your device.

No images or video frames are uploaded or transmitted to us. A page is saved only when you choose to capture it.

4. Photo Library Access

If you import an existing image or file, Scanzen accesses only the items you explicitly select. It does not scan or read your full photo library.

5. Your Documents & Files

Scanned documents, pages, signatures, and exported files (PDF, JPG, TXT, DOCX, PPTX) are stored locally on your device. Lakvanta Technologies has no access to them.

6. Text Recognition (OCR)

Optical character recognition runs on-device using Apple’s Vision framework. The text Scanzen extracts is processed locally and is never uploaded to our servers.

7. Subscriptions & Purchases

Scanzen Pro is offered through Apple’s In-App Purchase system. All billing is handled by Apple under your Apple ID. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Purchase validation uses Apple’s StoreKit and does not identify you to us personally.

8. Analytics & Diagnostics

Scanzen may use Apple-provided system diagnostics such as crash reports to improve stability and performance. These diagnostics are anonymous and do not include personal data or the content of your documents.

9. Third-Party Services

Scanzen does not use advertising networks, third-party analytics SDKs, or tracking services. The app relies only on Apple system frameworks such as VisionKit, Vision, PDFKit, and StoreKit.

10. Children’s Privacy

Scanzen does not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of child data misuse.

11. Data Security

Because Scanzen does not transmit or store your documents on external servers, your information remains under your control at all times.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Updates will be published on this page.
